Information About Me

I am a graduate of the University of Nigeria Nsukka(UNN), where i majored in Business Management. I hail from the eastern part of Nigeria, Anambra state, Aguata local government and Isoufia town to be more precise. Language has always been very intriguing for me and i think that is one of the major reason why i can now identify as a bilingual Web Developer not taking away the fact that i attended an awesome bilingual primary and secondary school in the city of Cotonou, Republic of Benin.
The journey to actually become a Web Developer was simply inspired by a Bollywood movie i watched during my early teenage days. Aamir Khan said, 'strive towards excellence and everything else will be gotten along the way', and this statement stuck with me and has always found a way to make me understand what activities i should prioritize in the quest for anything. Always aim to add value and with this, i am sure i can do such.

The Grace of God has seen me this far, and i am obviously nothing without Him. I pray God still guides me along the way and help bring me back everytime i falter. I am certainly with flaws but I am a passionate lover of Him.
